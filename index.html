About Me

I'm a software engineer with a keen interest in developing tools, pipelines and workflows to improve developer productivity. I strive to be a force multiplier, relentlessly seeking out opportunities to improve internal processes and eliminate workflow inefficiencies.

I'm currently an Engineer at Xero where I work on Accelerators, a set of standardised templates and tooling that help teams build new things faster. Prior to joining Xero, I was a DevOps Engineer at FIS where I maintained the continuous integration infrastructure and internal tooling used by over 200 engineers working on FIS Asset Finance, a large-scale FinTech .NET application.

In my free time, I enjoy playing games and honing my software engineering skills through personal projects, particularly in the area of real-time graphics.
