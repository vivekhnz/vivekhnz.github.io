Destiny Stats

About

Destiny Stats was an unofficial Windows Phone companion app for Bungie's video game, Destiny. Bungie developed an official Destiny companion app for iOS and Android but not for Windows Phone, which was my mobile platform of choice. I built Destiny Stats because I wanted a way to view my Guardian's stats and gear on my phone.

I began developing the app in July 2014 during a Destiny Beta maintenance window and released the first version of the app in August, in time for the game's launch in September. Over the next 14 months, I updated the app 15 times with enhancements to support the game's evolution. The app was downloaded over 100,000 times, received over 1,300 ratings and maintained a 4.4 / 5 star rating on the Windows Phone Store. It was also covered by Windows Central and Planet Destiny.
